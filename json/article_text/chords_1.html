Chords are extremely important for music composition. Chords can influence the mood of a song greatly, so using the right chords is extremely important.

Harmony

Chords are fundemental in establishing harmony between voices. This is because certain chords accomplish certain musical functions. This concept is the core of functional harmony. However, before using functional harmony to decide what chords to use, one must identify or decide the key of the piece. Note how all of the notes of the chords are built upon tones within the key, and not outside the key. The Roman numerals denote the relationship of the chord relative to the tonic. When the numerals are capitalized, a major chord is denoted; in lowercase, a minor chord is denoted. These Roman numeral names for the chords are used so that they can be applied to any tonic note.
