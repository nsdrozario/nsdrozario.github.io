Cadences are a special case of a chord progression, and are often parts of a chord progression. Cadences are a specific pattern of chords that lead one chord to another, usually the tonic chord.

Common cadences

  • Perfect cadence: V-I (major) or V-i (minor)
  • Plagal cadence: IV-I (major) or iv-i (minor)
  • Deceptive cadence: V-vi (major) or V-VI (minor)
